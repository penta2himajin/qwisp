### Part 1: The Conceptual Step-by-Step Explanation

Quicksort is a **Divide and Conquer** algorithm. It works by breaking a large problem into smaller sub-problems until they are trivial to solve.

#### Step 1: Choose a Pivot
The algorithm selects a specific element from the array to act as a "pivot" (or anchor). This element determines how the rest of the array is split.
*   **Common strategies:** Pick the first element, the last element, or a random element.
